# Image-Gen Prompts

Concept image only; not fabrication authority. Do not include cut-ready dimensions, hole locations, or toolpaths.

## Hero Prompt

A single finished soprano chalumeau presented as a real physical object: a slender, straight cylindrical single-reed folk pipe turned from warm close-grained hardwood, with a gently raised collar ringed around each finger tone hole along the front face and a row of evenly spaced holes that read as hand-reachable. One end carries a separate dark mouthpiece holding a flat cane single reed bound on with a clamp, and the opposite end opens into a modest turned flared bell of the same wood. The body shows subtle lathe-turned beading at the section joints between mouthpiece, body, and bell, hinting at the keyless first-build design before any metal levers are added. Surface is a satin oiled wood finish with visible natural grain, neutral seamless studio background, soft directional studio light grazing the body to reveal the tone-hole collars and turning detail, 3/4 product-photography angle, ~50mm lens, realistic table-scale proportions for a clarinet-relative woodwind, high detail, photorealistic, no text labels.

## In-Context Prompt

The same soprano chalumeau resting on a maker's workbench in a small wood shop, lying beside a coil of reed cane and a turned blank, with a lathe and hand tools softly blurred in the background; warm natural window light rakes across the oiled wood body and the raised tone-hole collars, shallow depth of field keeping the mouthpiece reed and front holes sharp while the shop falls into bokeh; environmental storytelling of a handmade folk-pipe prototype, concept visualization only, no text labels.

## Detail Prompt

Macro close-up of the chalumeau's dark mouthpiece and signature raised tone-hole collars: the flat cane single reed seated against the mouthpiece table and held by its clamp, transitioning into the cylindrical wood body where two or three turned collars rise around their finger holes, raking side light emphasizing the satin grain, the bevel of each collar, and the bound reed fibers; high detail, photorealistic, no measurements.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, logos, watermarks, or fake maker marks; no dimension callouts or measurement lines; no impossible mechanisms or floating keys; if a player is shown, anatomically plausible hands with fingers that plausibly cover the holes.
