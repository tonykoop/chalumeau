# Chalumeau Photo Shotlist

Current status: **starter shotlist; no Tony-built prototype photos are present**.

Use this list for future public-safe documentation. Attributed reference photos
in `assets/images/` are source/provenance references, not finished build proof.

## Before Build

- Chosen reed and mouthpiece with labels visible.
- Selected prototype blank with ruler and grain orientation.
- Drill, reamer, and bore tooling setup before cutting.
- Printed or screen-visible tone-hole schedule marked `prototype / TBD`.

## During Build

- Bore drilling or reaming setup with workholding visible.
- First body after turning, before tone-hole drilling.
- Undersized tone holes before tuning enlargement.
- Tuner/microphone setup with temperature and humidity notes.
- Keyless first sound test setup.
- Optional K01/K02 keywork parts before installation, if used.
- Leak-light or suction-test setup for any installed keys.

## Validation

- All-closed root measurement with reed/mouthpiece setup documented.
- Tone-hole sweep after each tuning pass.
- Any wax/tape/temporary correction used during tuning.
- Final measured hole diameters after prototype tuning.

## Finished Review

- Side, front, and bell-end views of the completed prototype.
- Mouthpiece/reed close-up.
- Tone-hole and optional keywork close-ups.
- Known defects or revisions needed before the next body.
